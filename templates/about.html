About BookSwap

Read more. Waste less. Connect more.

📘 What We Do

BookSwap is a community-driven platform that encourages readers to exchange books rather than let them gather dust. It’s about sharing stories, saving money, and nurturing a reading culture.

🔄 How It Works

List your books, browse collections, send swap requests, and exchange stories—literally. It’s effortless and rewarding.

🌟 Why Use BookSwap?

  • Explore new reads at no cost
  • Free up shelf space the eco-friendly way
  • Connect with fellow book lovers near you

✨ Behind the Project

BookSwap was born out of a shared desire to revive the books we love by giving them new lives in new hands.

🛠️ Powered By

  • Flask – routing and backend logic
  • MySQL – structured, secure data handling
  • Bootstrap – responsive design that works everywhere
